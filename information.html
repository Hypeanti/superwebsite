Information Page

    Event 1
  • Our first event is a gaming tournament. It features a plethora of popular games ranging from Fornite to League of Legends. People of all skill levels are welcome to join and just have a bit of fun in a friendly competitive environment.
  • There will be resources like controllers and headphones available for free use at the venue but it is recommended to bring your own. As well as this, there will be food and drink stands scattered across the venue where you can purchase only the finest quality refreshments to fuel your gaming endeavours.
    Event 2
  • Our second event will be an indie game convention. This event will give an opportunity to any up and coming game developers to showcase their projects and what they have been working on. The idea of this event is to help provide exposure to smaller developers and to potentially give them the boost they need to help their game take off. We aim to provide the best experience to all attending and to those showcasing their games.
  • As well we hope this can be an opportunity for game developers to have their games tested and played by others so they can get feedback on how their game plays.
  • The entire time during this event, there will be a "support stand" open throughout the whole time the event is on. The idea of this support stand is to provide help or guidance to any new developers or anyone at all who may need assistance with anything game development related.
    Event 3
  • Our third event will be a cosplay convention. This is a fun and welcoming event where people can cosplay as their favourite characters and show off their creativity and artistic talent. There is no limit to what you can cosplay as so we encourage you to bring your BEST cosplay and show off your hard work and passion.
